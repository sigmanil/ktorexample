Why doesn't App3TestImproved work?
 New threads (and coroutines) are started, and the threadlocal in our transactionmanager won't find the relevant
 transaction. So even though transactions are technically reentrant, they aren't in this case. We need to make sure
 each actual call is done within the overarching transaction.
